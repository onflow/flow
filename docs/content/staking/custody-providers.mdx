This section provides information for users who have acquired locked FLOW from the community sale, and are holding them in one of the following wallets.

Before reading this section, make sure you read the [overview](/staking/) to understand the concept of staking, delegating and earning rewards
on Flow.

## Blocto

For details about how to use your Blocto account to stake and earn rewards you'll
find everything you need to know in Blocto's [staking guide](https://guide.blocto.app/article/stake-flow-tokens-step-by-step-with-blocto).

#### Auto-restaking: Optional

With Blocto, users have the option to re-stake earned rewards, or not.

#### Delegating

Blocto allows users to delegate their locked FLOW as stake to a Flow node of their choice by providing the node's unique ID. This can be done using the Flow Port.
For details about how to delegate using Flow Port, please see our [step-by-step](/) guide.

#### Withdrawing Rewards

Users using the Blocto wallet can also withdraw their rewards using the Flow Port!

## Ledger

If you're storing your locked FLOW tokens using a Ledger device, you can use the Flow Port to **stake** or **delegate** them to earn rewards.
For more details you can read [this official post](https://www.onflow.org/post/flow-port-is-here) to familiarize yourself with the Flow Port app,
and how to use it as a staking portal.

#### Auto-restaking: No

Flow Port does not support auto-restaking of your rewards. Follow the Flow Port [staking guide](/support/flow-port/staking-guide/) for instructions about how to stake using the Port.

#### Delegating

Follow the Flow Port [staking guide](/support/flow-port/staking-guide/) for instructions about how to delegate stake using the Port.

#### Withdrawing Rewards

Use [Flow Port](https://port.onflow.org) to withdraw any rewards you earned by staking or delegating locked FLOW form your Ledger device.

## Coinlist

Coinlist is operating FLOW nodes and will automatically delegate your locked FLOW to them as stake.
If you would like to withdraw your rewards, you'll need to contact Coinlist's support team.

#### Auto-restaking: Yes

Coinlist will automatically re-stake any rewards you earn. If you'd like to withdraw or delegate stake to another node, you'll need to
contact Coinlist's support team by email.

#### Delegating

If you would like to delegate our locked FLOW to another node of your choosing, you'll need to contact Coinlist's support team.

#### Withdrawing Rewards

If you would like to withdraw your rewards, you'll need to contact Coinlist's support team.

## Kraken

Kraken is operating FLOW nodes and will automatically delegate your locked FLOW to them as stake.
For details about how to delegate, or withdraw your rewards, you'll need to contact Kraken's support team by email.

#### Auto-restaking: Yes

Kraken will automatically re-stake any rewards you earn.

#### Delegating

If you would like to delegate your locked FLOW to another node of your choosing, you'll need to contact Kraken's support team.

#### Withdrawing Rewards

If you would like to withdraw your rewards, you'll need to contact Kraken's support team.
